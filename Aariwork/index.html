Welcome to Lotus Aari Work

Welcome to Lotus Aari Works, a leading company in the field of Aari Work with over 5 years of experience. Our company is dedicated to providing professional Aari embroidery training to students all over the world. We have had the privilege of training over 500 students in our time, and we are proud to offer our courses in two languages (Tamil and English) to make them accessible to a wider experience.

We provide the following classes

  • Aari Embroidery Class
  • Aari Tracing Workshop
  • Saree Pre Pleating Class
  • Fabric Painting Class
