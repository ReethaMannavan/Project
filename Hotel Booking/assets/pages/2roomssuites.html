ROOMS & SUITES

Premier Suite Room

with a view that rivals the most romantic novels, tranquil camelia with its big glass window opening to the scenic valley, offers facilities like 32inch TV, minibar, hair dryer, iron box, safe locker, air - conditioning and Wifi. The room with the floor area of 285 square feet, comes with a cubicle wash room replete with all the basic toiletries. You can indulge in the cool breeze that blows from the mountains and sip on a warm beverage with your loved one by your side as you take in the ethereal beauty of Munnar and its Mountains.

Family Suites

A Spacious room with 543 square feet of the floor space, The tranquil premier gives you a picture postcard view from the balcony of the river, tea plantations and the valley. It has a host of modern facilities like a 42 inch LED TV, minibar, hair dryer, ironbox, safe lockers, air-conditioning and Wi-Fi. Graced with a queen sized bed and cubicle shower bathroom , the room will make you feel spoiled and wishing you could stay that bit longer.

Honeymoon Suite

This is a honeymooner dreams comes true. Offering an experience that you should definitely not miss out on is the Blanket Honeymoon Pavillion. with it's spacious 400 square feet floor area, the room is ideal for a honeymoon couple or those looking for a romantic getaway. with basic amenities like 40 inch Tv , mini bar, hair dryer, iron box, safe locker, air - conditioning and Wi-Fi. The room offers an exclusive private balcony that gives couples a 180 degree picturesque and the panoramic view of the mountains, waterfalls, river, tea plantations and the valley.

Presidential Suites

The Presidential suite is a room that fits the name. A room that is fit for a royalty, it comes with a jacuzzi that offers a view of the valley, the majestic attukad falls, river and lush green tea plantations. It redefines luxury with a host of modern facilities and you can be rest assured of nothing short of an experience that leaves you wanting more. There is a coffee machine in the living room for those who are always in need of their caffeine shots. A cubicle shower washroom and other basic amenities like 42 inch TV, Mini bar, hair dryer, iron box, safe locker, air - conditioning and Wi-Fi.
