TREKKING WITH OUR IN HOUSE NATURALIST

You can choose to trek with our in house naturalist who will take you through the tea plantations all the way upto the Attakad waterfalls. On the way you will come across people from the local community who work at the tree plantations. Scenes of Women delicately plucking tea leaves to the plantations watcher who keeps a close eye on everything and everyone with his bag mustache and cane are all the sights that greet you on your trek to the falls. In the distance, you can watch the beautiful sight of the waterfalls dropping like a stream of milk over the rocks. Once you reach the falls you can take in the picturesque beauty and breath in the fresh air. please be advised that entering the falls or river is dangerous due to the presence of strong undercurrent and the underwater troughs. it is best to refrain from such activities.

YOGA & MEDITATION

The ancient practice of Yoga and Meditation has stood the test of time and continue to prove its worth over modern medicine in terms of the long term mental and physical well being of those who practice the lifestyle prescribed by the tenets of yoga. As part of our efforts to include a habit of healthy living, We at Blanket Hotel and spa provide our guests with an opportunity to partake in a yoga session with our in house yoga instructor in the morning.

CHEF'S CORNER

This activity provides a guest with platform to showcase their culinary skills and talent. Guests are alos welcome to cook with the chef and sample the dishes they have prepared. Also they can teach the chef a thing or two about their favourite dish and it's recipe. As a prize the executive chef rewards the guests with the "Chef of the Day" prize and their snaps will be posted the next day on the social media page of our resort.

ROMANTIC DINNER AT RIVERSIDE

At evening where guests can watch the night sky by being in the company of their loved ones and ignite the romance in their lives by having a candle light dinner and complete surrendering to the sense of bliss that the entire evening offers. They can indulge themselves to their's heart content in this very romantic setting and listen to some romantic ghazals while creating moments that can be framed and cherised as memories for years to come.
